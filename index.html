About Me

Hi! I'm Sayad Md Bayezid, a technology entrepreneur and digital marketing expert based in Tangail, Bangladesh.

I'm the founder of CWB Agency (Connect With Bayezid), GenZFrontier (digital news portal), and SmartGen (QR code generator).

My expertise includes:
